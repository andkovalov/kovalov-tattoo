Privacy
policy

This policy describes what personal data I collect through the kovalov.tattoo website, the purposes for which I process it, and what rights you have regarding it.

Last updated: 29 May 2025

01

Data controller

The data controller is Andrey Kovalov, a tattoo artist based in Prague (the "controller"). The controller processes personal data in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council (GDPR) and Act No. 110/2019 Coll. on personal data processing.

The controller's contact details are at the end of this document.

02

What data I process

I process only the data you provide yourself, or that arises during normal operation of the site:

  • Contact details — name, phone, email or Telegram / WhatsApp username that you enter in the form.
  • Enquiry content — the idea description, placement, size and other details about the planned tattoo that you provide in the brief.
  • Technical data — anonymised data about your visit (device type, browser, approximate location), if you consent via cookies.
03

Purpose and legal basis

I process your data solely for the following purposes:

  • Handling your enquiry — so I can contact you, arrange a consultation and prepare a design. The legal basis is steps toward entering a contract.
  • Traffic analytics and marketing — to understand how the site is used. The legal basis is your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

I never sell your data or use it for purposes other than those stated here.

04

Cookies

The site uses cookies. Necessary cookies are always active; the rest only with your consent, which you manage via the consent bar or the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

Necessary Always active

They ensure the basic functioning of the site and store your cookie choice. Without them the site doesn't work properly.

Analytics With consent

They anonymously measure traffic and behaviour on the site so I can improve it.

Marketing With consent

They allow relevant content to be shown and measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns.

05

Retention period

I keep contact details and enquiry content for as long as necessary to handle the enquiry and then for the duration of our relationship, but no longer than 2 years from the last contact. Data processed on the basis of consent is kept until you withdraw it.

06

Sharing with third parties

For running the site and communication I use trusted service providers (notably the Telegram and WhatsApp messengers and analytics tools). These processors handle data only on my instructions and in line with GDPR. I don't share data with anyone else.

07

Your rights

Regarding your personal data, under GDPR you have the right:

  • to access your data and obtain a copy,
  • to rectify inaccurate or complete incomplete data,
  • to erasure ("the right to be forgotten"),
  • to restrict processing,
  • to data portability,
  • to object to processing and withdraw consent at any time,
  • to lodge a complaint with the Office for Personal Data Protection (uoou.gov.cz).

Contact

Have a question about data protection or want to exercise one of your rights? Message me — I'll reply as soon as possible.
